Pizza Margherita

Step-by-Step Guide

Ingredients

  • 275g water
  • 25g yeast
  • 425g wheat flour tipo 00 - very important!
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • pizzasauce preferably this homemade sauce
  • 4 tomatoes
  • oregano dried
  • basil fresh
  • mozzarella

Instructions

  1. Start by making the pizza dough. It's easy. Find your mixer and mix water and yeast.
  2. Knead then the wheat flour tipo 00 in the dough.
  3. Put the dough on the counter and knead it. Here's an instruction on how to do it.
  4. Put the dough back in the mixer bowl. Put a moist piece of cloth on top and let it raise for 45 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 225 degrees celsius hot air.
  6. Line a baking pan with baking paper.
  7. Take the dough out onto the table and quickly knead it again. Roll it out so that it almost fits the size of your roasting pan. If necessary, pull out the last piece of dough so that it fills the entire baking pan. The edges of your pizza should preferably be a little thicker.
  8. Spread a thin layer of pizza sauce on the pizza, then spread plum tomatoes cut into smaller boats. Drizzle with a little olive oil and sprinkle with some dried oregano.
  9. Bake your Pizza Margherita for 15 minutes at 225 degrees hot air.
  10. Take your pizza pan out of the oven and spread slices of mozzarella and 10 basil leaves on it. Season with a little salt and freshly ground pepper and a bit more dried oregano, and now bake the pizza for another eight minutes so that it is crispy and delicious.
